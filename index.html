The Creativitea Anthology

CreativiTea is a creative writing student organization at Virginia Tech. It was founded during Spring 2019. It is a non-judgemental space for writers to learn from and share their own writings. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, we are publishing our very first anthology - collection of student works - online. To learn more about CreativiTea and what we do, click the button below!
